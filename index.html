My Work




MQUAP IS

Multi Qualification Acquisition Program – Intelligent System of the University of Nueva Caceres.

This project will help the teacher faculty with matching certifications to their courses by our system.

Our system will determine a percentage match of certification content and course content with text matching.

Text matching is powered by 2 algorithms (1) Brute Force String Pattern Matching: to search for common words between the course and certification, and (2)Cosine Similarity to calculate the percentage match.
